This is a personal passion project that seemed quite intresting. I love the design of the Cobalt 60 capsles and thought it would be a good novalty water bottle.
This was also a chance to work with threads, engraving, and economical value of water bottles.
I grasped a better understanding of how water bottle are made and why they use thin cheap aluminum. 
Weight, price, and structure all played a role, I focused on making it extremely duriable yet found it wasn't cost effecient. 
This gave me insite into water proof threading as well. Thread design can change how the structure does against torque, sealing, and durabilty.
